# Niche & Audience

## Niche
Vibe coding, Claude / Claude Code, AI dev tools, build-in-public, indie founders, startups, digital-nomad lifestyle.

## Who it's for (ICP)
Solo founders and indie builders who are **early in their AI journey** — they use (or want to use) AI to build and ship, consume short-form content on Instagram, and care about both the *tools* and the *lifestyle*. They want practical, slightly-ahead-of-the-curve signal they can act on, not deep research.

## What makes a good topic for a Reel
- Concrete + demoable (a tool, a trick, a number)
- Slightly ahead of what the audience already knows
- Emotional hook available: money saved, contrarian take, "you're doing it wrong", FOMO
- Ties back to *building* or the *founder lifestyle*
